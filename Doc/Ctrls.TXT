To create a TempleOS graphic ctrl, you define callback functions and insert a 
[C:/Kernel/KernelA.HPP.Z,3010] CCtrl structure in the
[C:/Kernel/KernelA.HPP.Z,3218] CTask queue.  See
[C:/Demo/Graphics/Slider.CPP.Z,1], 
[C:/Demo/Graphics/ScrollBars.CPP.Z,1] and [C:/Adam/WallPaper.CPP.Z,1]
TermButtonNew.  There is a template-code 
ctrl generator, if you press <CTRL-SHIFT-L>.
