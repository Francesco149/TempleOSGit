To create a TempleOS graphic ctrl, you define call-back functions and insert
a 
[C:/TempleOS/Kernel/Adam1a.HPP.Z,2834] CCtrl structure in the
[C:/TempleOS/Kernel/Adam1a.HPP.Z,3008] CTask queue.  See
[C:/Demo/Graphics/SliderCtrl.CPP,1], 
[C:/Demo/Graphics/ScrollBars.CPP,1] and [C:/Adam/WallPaper.CPP,1]
TermButtonNew.  There is a managed-code 
ctrl generator, if you press <CTRL-SHIFT-L>.
