To create a TempleOS graphic ctrl, you define call-back functions and insert
a 
[C:/Kernel/Adam1a.HPP.Z,2680] CCtrl structure in the
[C:/Kernel/Adam1a.HPP.Z,2866] CTask queue.  See
[C:/Demo/Graphics/SliderCtrl.CPP.Z,1], 
[C:/Demo/Graphics/ScrollBars.CPP.Z,1] and [C:/Adam/WallPaper.CPP.Z,1]
TermButtonNew.  There is a managed-code 
ctrl generator, if you press <CTRL-SHIFT-L>.
