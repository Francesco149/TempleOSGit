One jiffy is one time slice.  [C:/LT/OSMain/Adam1b.HPZ,210] sys_jiffies
returns time slices since boot.

[C:/LT/OSMain/Misc1a.CPZ,63] SysTimerRead reads the timer ticks since
boot.  It's not as fast as 
[C:/LT/OSMain/Adam1b.HPZ,202] GetTimeStamp.

Use [C:/LT/OSMain/Adam1a.HPZ,465] JIFFY_FREQ to convert
[C:/LT/OSMain/Adam1b.HPZ,210] sys_jiffies.

Use [C:/LT/OSMain/Adam1a.HPZ,467] SYS_TIMER_FREQ to convert
[C:/LT/OSMain/Misc1a.CPZ,63] SysTimerRead.
