To create a TempleOS graphic ctrl, you define call-back functions and insert
a 
[C:/TempleOS/Kernel/Adam1a.HPP.Z,2812] CCtrl structure in the
[C:/TempleOS/Kernel/Adam1a.HPP.Z,2987] CTask queue.  See 
[C:/TempleOS/Demo/Graphics/SliderCtrl.CPP.Z,1], 
[C:/TempleOS/Demo/Graphics/ScrollBars.CPP.Z,1] and
[C:/TempleOS/Adam/WallPaper.CPP.Z,1] TermButtonNew.  There is a 
managed-code ctrl generator, if you press <CTRL-SHIFT-L>.
